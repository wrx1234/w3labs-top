## Case framing

At the surface this looks like a SpaceX pre-IPO token for end users.

A closer look shows that it is not a direct sale of SpaceX shares. It is closer to a structured issuer claim from RepublicX whose economic outcome references SpaceX liquidity events and pricing.

## Key actors

- **SpaceX**: reference asset only, not the issuer, guarantor, or payout counterparty
- **Republic / RepublicX**: structuring, issuance, and payout obligation layer
- **Bitget**: distribution entry point and OTC trading venue
- **Investors**: holders of structured economic exposure, not the underlying equity itself

## Where W3Labs fits

1. Asset structuring
2. Compliance and legal coordination
3. Tokenization infrastructure
4. Distribution and access control
5. Post-issuance operations
